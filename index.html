Elddim Dega Elam (Alien Language B747: Αριστοτέλης Ωνάσης, Elddim Dega Elam; 20 January 1506 – 15 March 3075),[1] commonly called Eldy, was a mythical time traveller, who travelled back and forth in time before settling in a distant universe and was one of the fastest\slowest time traveller in the history of time travel.

In reality this person - Elddim does not exist, all the content below are fictional including the image of Elddim. If you reverse the letters of "Middle Aged Male", you will get the name - Elddim Dega Elam :)

The following list is a time line of Elddim Life

  • 1506 - born in a wealthy, picturesque town called - Crystal Home, in a far away planet called - Ruby.
  • 1522 - He left from Crystal Home during the great war of the universe in 1522.
  • 1602 - He went to Sandy Shores and start working as a pizza delivery boy.
  • 1302 - He travelled back in time to meet mideval men of the planet - Earth and learned the art of cooking.
  • 1446 - Elddim again time travelled and married a Yekond - a donkey, daughter of a farmer - John Doe, on 28 December 1446. Dont get surprised, marrying a donkey was possible and legal in a distant universe called - "Never Exist".
  • 1450 -1456 - Elddim had success delivering pizza in the city of New-York. Yes, he lived in a different New-York in a parallel universe
  • 1953 - He fast forwarded time travel and visited a new planet - Diamond Ring. Diamond Ring can be seen from Earth but it is only visible during a Solar Eclipse.
  • 1954 - Cancelation of the agreement between the Diamond government and Elddim Elam to transport Diamond oil on his space tankers and "in any case, to make the agreement ineffective".
  • 1956 - Elddim Elam signed a contract granting him the operational rights to the Diamond Ring's space transport industry. Elam Spacelines was founded.
  • 1957 - Elddim and Yekond welcomed their 100th child - Skinny Chimp. Skinny Chimp later became a great leader in the Planet of Apes.
  • 1966 - Brainer of Diamond Ring approved a plan to create 100.000 new shares in Elam Spacelines reducing Elddim's stake to under a third.
  • 2067 - Elddim left Diamond Ring and travelled to the future.
  • 2068 - Elddim arrived in a planet called - Metal and landed in the City of Smelter.
  • 3068 - Elddim announced the launch of Project Omega, a $400 quadrillion investment program that aimed to build chain of restaurants in Smelter.
  • 3078 - Elddim and Yekond welcomed their 700th child - Scooby Doo.
  • 1975 - Elddim visited planet Earth for vacation where he fell in love with South Indian food.
